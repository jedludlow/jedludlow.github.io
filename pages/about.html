Experience

I develop high-performance, multidisciplinary systems for energy, medical, and aerospace applications. Representative projects include

  • Wearable sensing systems
  • Implantable rotary blood pump with a magnetically levitated impeller
  • Specialized accelerometers for inertial navigation systems

Electronic Systems
